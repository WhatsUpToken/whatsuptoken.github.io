About

WHATSUP is a deflationary DeFi token built on the Binance Smart Chain.

The main idea behind WHATSUP is to provide a decentralized transaction network which operates on the Binance Smart Chain. It's a fun community driven project with a strong roadmap in place to ensure successful adoption of the token.

WHATSUP is a deflationary token with a max circulating supply of 1 Quadrillion. On each transaction, a tax of 3% will be distributed to the holders and a further 3% will be burnt to the LP, hence incentivizing holders to prevent price manipulation and decreasing the supply overtime. As the supply decreases, the scarcity of the token increases. This inversely-proportional relationship constitutes a supply and demand model. Furthermore, there is no limit as to how many tokens can be burnt. Without a burning limit you know what happens next.
